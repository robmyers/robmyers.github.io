</br>pure:dyne is an operating system developed to provide media artists with
</br>a complete set of tools for realtime audio and video processing.
</br>pure:dyne is a live distribution, you don’t need to install anything.
</br>Simply boot your computer using the liveCD/DVD or liveUSB and you’re
</br>ready to start using software such as Pure Data, Supercollider, Icecast,
</br>Csound, Fluxus, Processing, Arduino and much much more.
</br>
</br>pure:dyne will work on any x86 PC laptop, desktop, and single-board
</br>computers, including the intel-based Mac, Asus’ Eee PC, and any x86
</br>netbooks :)

</br>Each pure:dyne release come with a Free/Libre and Open Source Soup
</br>(FLOSS). This time we hope you will enjoy our leek and potato flavor :)
</br>
</br>Depends: Leek, Potato, Milk, Salt, Oil, Pumpkin oil
</br>Suggests: Thyme, Sage, Pepper, Parsley, Crème fraiche
</br>Build-Depends: Cookpot, BlenderkMasher, Frypan, Bowl
</br>
</br>make: default: serving
</br>tender potato: potato cookpot salt water 20min
</br>tasty leek: leek oil frypan 5min
</br>puree: tender potato tasty leek blender
</br>soup: puree milk herbs cookpot 5min
</br>herbs: thyme sage pepper serving: soup bowl
</br>parsely pumpkin oil crème fraiche
