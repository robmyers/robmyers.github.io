Aesthetic Aura Investigation One

30 May 2007

Hypothesis: The aesthetic aura of art as identified by Walter Benjamin will result in anomalous electromagnetic field readings around artworks. This will vary according to the aesthetic quality of the artwork. We base this claim on results from paranormal research. A-E is the aesthetic quality of the work, where E is low and A is high.
