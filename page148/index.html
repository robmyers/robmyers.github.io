The Free Culture Foundation has commissioned a set of essays examining issues at the heart of the free culture movement, to be published in April and May. These essays, by artists, activists and academics will provide the coherent, accessible introduction to the free culture movement that the FCF promised upon its launch.

What is the best Free Culture license? at Understanding

27 Mar 2007

What is the best Free Culture license? at Understanding

I’m not sure what the best free non-software license is. That is, a license that respects the public’s freedom for things which are software, but not functional software - a digital manual for a program, not the program software itself, for example.

I’m sure who writes the most popular licenses used in this way is, though: Creative Commons.
